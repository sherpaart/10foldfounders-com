How it works

A clear path, one phase at a time.

10Fold Founders runs on the same system we use to scale businesses. It becomes your journey. A new group starts each month and moves one phase at a time. Work the order and you end with a business that runs without you. Then you stay, because finishing is the start, not the exit.

Each phase builds on the one before it. You are never doing it alone, and you always know exactly where you are.
